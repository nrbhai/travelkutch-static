Overview

Dhordo is the gateway to the world-famous White Rann of Kutch‚Äîa vast salt marsh that transforms into a shimmering surreal landscape under the moonlight. Once a quiet hamlet, it is now the epicenter of the Rann Utsav (Nov-Feb), hosting the magnificent Tent City. Whether you want to witness a sunrise where the salt meets the sky or experience Kutchi culture at its vibrant best, Dhordo is the place to be. It serves as both a destination and a base for exploring the northern desert region.
